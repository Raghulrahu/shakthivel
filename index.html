    VP Packaging located in chengalpattu is a leading design, printing & packaging company manufacturing wide range of corrugated boxes, printed cartons and providing package design solutions to a wide array of industry applications.


    The operations are run by Mr.Vignesh & Mr.Shakthivel.


    We make about 25 million regular sized 3 ply boxes a year. Our plant has some of the latest machinery available for converting and corrugating. VP Packaging is the largest corrugator in Chengalpattu areas.


    The Plant is also equipped with the imported multi color flexographic and rotary die cutting/punching machines.
